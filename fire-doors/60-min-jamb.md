# 60-Minute Fire Door Frame (Jamb) SOP

## PURPOSE
To define the standardized process for manufacturing and machining 60-minute rated wood/composite fire door frames in compliance with FyreWerks Intertek Category C listings.

## RESPONSIBILITY
- Engineering: Define design  
- Production: Build frame  
- Machining: Perform prep  
- QC: Verify compliance  

---

## PROCEDURE

### 1. Core Selection
1. Use FF or FFX core  
2. Match work order  

---

### 2. Construction
1. Frame thickness ≥ 3/4"  
2. Frame depth ≥ 4-5/8"  
3. Follow joint rules  

---

### 3. Rabbet & Stop
1. Rabbet ≥ 1-7/8"  
2. Stop ≥ 1/2"  
3. Stops at 12" OC  

---

### 4. Face Materials
1. Veneer ≥ 18 pcf  
2. Thickness limits apply  

---

### 5. Edge Banding
1. Max 3/16"  

---

### 6. Jamb Extensions
1. Allowed per spec  

---

### 7. Adhesives
1. Fire-rated only  

---

### 8. Fire Features
1. Edge sealing required for FFX  
2. Intumescent must be continuous  

---

### 9. Machining
1. Hinges and strike prep only  

---

### 10. Labeling
1. Apply certification label  

---

### 11. QC
1. Verify all requirements  

---

## EXPECTATION

- Frame is a fire system  
- Edge sealing is critical  
- No deviations allowed

# Engineering Standard – Sheppard (In-Wall) Brackets with Side-to-Side Adjustability

## PURPOSE
To standardize the engineering of Sheppard (in-wall) brackets to include side-to-side adjustability, allowing for minor field scribing without requiring bracket remounting.

## RESPONSIBILITY
- **Engineering Team**: Incorporate adjustability into all applicable bracket designs and shop drawings.
- **Project Managers (PMs)**: Ensure project scope and installation conditions are communicated to engineering.
- **Shop and Installation Teams**: Use the adjustability as intended during installation and report any limitations or issues.

## Procedure

1. **Bracket Design Requirements**
   1. All Sheppard (in-wall) brackets shall include side-to-side adjustability.
   2. Design must allow for a total tolerance of **±1/8" horizontal adjustment**.

2. **Engineering Implementation**
   1. Incorporate slotted holes, oversized holes, or equivalent hardware solutions to achieve the required adjustment range.
   2. Ensure adjustability does not compromise structural integrity or load requirements.
   3. Clearly indicate adjustable features in shop drawings and details.

3. **Shop Drawing Documentation**
   1. Call out:
      - Adjustment range (**±1/8"**)
      - Fastener type and size
      - Slot or hole dimensions
   2. Include installation notes stating that brackets are designed for minor scribing adjustments without remounting.

4. **Coordination with Installation**
   1. Communicate the intent of adjustability to installers.
   2. Ensure installation sequence allows adjustment before final fastening.

5. **Quality Check**
   1. Engineering review must confirm adjustability is included before release.
   2. QC must verify fabricated brackets match engineered adjustability details.

## Expectation
All engineered Sheppard (in-wall) brackets shall provide **±1/8" side-to-side adjustment** so installers can make minor scribing adjustments without removing or remounting the bracket, reducing field rework and improving installation efficiency.

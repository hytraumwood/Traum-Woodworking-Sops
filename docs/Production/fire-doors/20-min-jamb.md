# 20-Minute Fire Door Frame (Jamb) SOP

## PURPOSE
To define the standardized process for manufacturing and machining 20-minute rated wood fire door frames in compliance with FyreWerks Category C listings.

## RESPONSIBILITY
- Engineering: Define frame design  
- Production: Build frames  
- Machining: Perform prep  
- QC: Verify compliance  

---

## PROCEDURE

### 1. Materials
1. Use approved wood species  
2. No finger joints allowed  

---

### 2. Construction
1. Jamb ≥ 3/4"  
2. Stop height = 1/2"  
3. Rabbet per spec  

---

### 3. Assembly
1. Frame square  
2. Joints tight  

---

### 4. Installation Interface
1. Wall thickness ≥ 3-1/2"  
2. Shim ≤ 1/2"  

---

### 5. Gasketing
1. Install approved gasketing  

---

### 6. Machining
1. Hinges and strike prep only  

---

### 7. Labeling
1. Apply WHI label  

---

### 8. QC
1. Verify materials and dimensions  

---

## EXPECTATION

- Gasketing is critical  
- No deviations allowed

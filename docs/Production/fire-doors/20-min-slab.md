# 20-Minute Slab Fire Door SOP

## PURPOSE
Define the standardized process for manufacturing 20-minute rated slab fire doors in compliance with listing requirements.

## RESPONSIBILITY
- Engineering: Ensure design meets listing
- Production: Build per specification
- Machining: Perform compliant prep
- QC: Verify compliance and labeling

## Procedure

### 1. Pre-Production
1. Verify door size and thickness meet listing
2. Confirm core type and materials per work order

### 2. Core & Materials
1. Use approved core material
2. Verify no prohibited joints
3. Use approved adhesive

### 3. Layup & Pressing
1. Complete layup per specification
2. Press per adhesive requirements
3. Ensure no voids or delamination

### 4. Faces & Edges
1. Apply face materials per spec
2. Apply edge banding if required
3. Ensure no exposed core

### 5. Machining
1. Perform hinge and lock prep within NFPA 80 limits
2. No unapproved machining

### 6. Labeling
1. Apply fire label after QC approval

## Expectation
- No deviations from listing
- Door must be square, flat, and defect-free
- Non-compliant doors must not be labeled

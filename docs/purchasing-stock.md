# SOP - Purchasing & Request Process for Stock Items

## PURPOSE
Define the process for purchasing stock materials and requesting them for use on the shop floor.

## RESPONSIBILITY
- **Purchasing Agent**: Purchase stock materials for inventory
- **Purchasing Manager**: Receive and distribute inventory
- **Shop Floor**: Request and pull materials from inventory
- **Stockbiller/Engineering**: Ensure materials are properly flagged as stock

## Procedure

### 1. Stock Material Setup
1.1 Materials designated as stock must be:
- Marked as **Stock Items** on the Material Card in Innergy

---

### 2. Purchasing Stock Items
2.1 Purchasing reviews the **MTB Grid (Stock View)**

2.2 Purchase materials for:
- Inventory replenishment
- General shop use (not tied to a specific work order)

---

### 3. Receiving Stock Items
3.1 All stock materials are:
- **Received into Inventory** (not to a work order)

---

### 4. Requesting Stock Items (Shop Floor)
4.1 To pull materials from inventory, the shop must:

- Use **Request & Fulfill**, OR  
- Submit a **written ticket** including:
  - Material description
  - Quantity
  - Work order (if applicable)

4.2 Ticket must be given to:
- **Purchasing Manager / Purchasing Department**

---

### 5. Fulfillment
5.1 Purchasing fulfills request by:
- Pulling material from inventory
- Allocating to the correct work order (if required)

---

## Expectation
- All stock items are purchased through the MTB Grid
- Inventory is accurately maintained
- No materials are taken without request/fulfillment
- Shop communicates all needs clearly through the defined process

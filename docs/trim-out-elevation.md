# SOP - Shop Fit-Up Process

## PURPOSE

Ensure assemblies fit correctly before leaving the shop to reduce install issues and rework.

## RESPONSIBILITY

* **Shop (Assembly)**: Perform fit-up
* **Engineering**: Provide clear drawings
* **Project Management**: Verify requirement is met when needed

## Procedure

1. Identify assemblies that require fit-up:

   * Multi-cabinet runs
   * Large/tall elevations
   * Complex joinery or alignment conditions

2. Dry-fit components in the shop:

   * Assemble parts to confirm fit and alignment
   * Verify joints, reveals, and overall dimensions

3. Adjust as needed:

   * Correct any fit, alignment, or tolerance issues

4. Document when required:

   * Take photos of assembled elevation if requested

5. Disassemble and prep for shipment

## Expectation

* Fit-up is completed on all critical assemblies
* Issues are resolved in-shop, not in the field
* Assemblies leave the shop ready for efficient installation

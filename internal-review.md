# SOP - Internal Review of Shop Drawings

## PURPOSE
Ensure all shop drawings are reviewed internally for accuracy and completeness prior to client submittal.

## RESPONSIBILITY
- **Draftsperson**: Initiate and revise drawings
- **Project Manager (PM)**: Review and provide redlines
- **Head of Engineering**: Review and provide redlines

## Procedure

### 1. Initial Review Notification
1.1 Draftsperson notifies:
- Project Manager
- Head of Engineering

1.2 Provide drawing set for review

---

### 2. Redline Collection
2.1 PM and Head of Engineering:
- Review drawings
- Provide redlines/comments

2.2 Draftsperson collects and consolidates all feedback

---

### 3. Revision
3.1 Draftsperson updates drawings based on redlines

---

### 4. Final Internal Check
4.1 Revised drawing set is sent back to:
- Project Manager
- Head of Engineering

4.2 Both parties confirm drawings are ready for submittal

---

### 5. Submittal Process
5.1 Once approved internally:
- PM proceeds with [Submittal](/submittals.md) process

---

## Expectation
- No drawings are submitted without both reviews
- All redlines are addressed prior to submittal
- Final set is verified before release
- PM tracks submittal status per standard process

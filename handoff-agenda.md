# Handoff
This SOP details and structures the handoff meeting between Engineering and Production

## When
Weekly meeting scheduled; cancelled if there are no new releases to the shop to walk through

## Who 
- Shop leadership; mill, sheets, assembly, finish 
  - veneering if decided necessary
- Engineering
  - Project Manager of release(s)
  - Stockbiller of release(s)
  - Drafter

## Agenda
- **Releases ordered due to critical date/ release order**

- Critical dates; any hard dates, any special deadlines, expedite, etc.
- Scope overview; 
  - Overall details:
    - Stile and rail doors or flat panel
    - Solids vs veneer
  - Purchasing
    - Long lead items/order status
    - Needs for lumber/veneer; order status
  - Finish samples brought to meeting so the shop understands finish before release
  - Workflow type; cabinetry, paneling, doors, etc.
  - Special considerations for scope; glass, leather, fabric, metal, etc. 
- Details
  - Profiles, drawer fronts, interactions
- Shop breakdown
  - Should this scope area/room be released to the shop in separate releases?
  - What is the breakdown?
- Special considerations

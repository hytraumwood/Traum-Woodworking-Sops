# SOP- Drawing Review Process

## PURPOSE: To reduce the number of human errors passed downstream; to the client, to stockbilling, to the shop, to the installer, etc.
## RESPONSIBILITY: Project Managers/Coordinators

## Procedure:
1. Check the [[naming convention]] for the shop drawing
    1. Revision correct?
    2. Submittal stage correct? (Submittal, Approval, RFC)
2. Check the following information on the cover page
    1. Revision catalog- correct?
    2. Submittal stage- correct?
    3. Floor Plan on cover page
    4. Material Schedule
    5. Hardware Schedule

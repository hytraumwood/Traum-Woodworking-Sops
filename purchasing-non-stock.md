# SOP - Purchasing Process for Non-Stock Items

## PURPOSE
Ensure all non-stock materials are properly tracked, purchased, and received using a standardized process.

## RESPONSIBILITY
- **Stockbiller**: Create material cards and assign to BOM
- **Purchasing Agent**: Generate and issue POs
- **Purchasing Manager**: Receive materials correctly
- **Engineering/PM**: Ensure materials are identified

## Procedure

### 1. Material Setup
1.1 Every purchased material must have a **Material Card in Innergy**

1.2 Material must be added to the **Bill of Material (BOM)** for the work order

1.3 If material does NOT exist in Innergy:
- Stockbiller creates card with minimum required info
- Purchasing completes/enhances card during quote/purchase

1.4 Drawer requests:
- Must be placed in the **Work Order Files tab** for purchasing to retrieve

---

### 2. Materials to Buy (MTB)
2.1 The **MTB Grid is the single source of truth** for all non-stock purchasing

2.2 All materials must flow through:
- Material Card → BOM → MTB Grid

---

### 3. Purchasing Process
3.1 Purchasing reviews MTB Grid

3.2 Generate Purchase Orders by:
- Vendor grouping
- Selecting **“Create PO by Work Order”**

---

### 4. Receiving
4.1 All non-stock items are:
- **Received directly to the Work Order**

---

### 5. Overages (Stock Allocation)
5.1 If purchasing extra quantity (e.g., buying 40 when 4 are needed):

- Required quantity → Work Order  
- Excess quantity → Inventory

5.2 Overages must be:
- **Allocated on the PO BEFORE submission**
- **Received correctly into inventory**

---

## Expectation
- MTB Grid is always accurate and up to date
- No materials are purchased outside this process
- Work orders receive correct quantities
- Overages are properly allocated and tracked

# Drafting Kanban- Labor Card
This SOP determines who/when a drafting labor kanban card is created, completed, etc.

## Creation
- The labor kanban card for drafting is [created](file here) in the linked process. Reminder: at least one hour of drafting labor must be attached to a work order in order for it to show up in the Labor Kanban
## Queue
- The card is moved to the assigned drafter
- Queue is reviewed in the weekly [engineering meeting](file here)
- The drafter works on the shop drawings in descending order in their queue
  - Note: submittals process no longer captured in engineering meeting. Find submittals process [here](/submittals.md)

## Closing
- When an RFC drawing is published, the drafter must move the card for the room to "drafting complete" labor step in the kanban
- The project manager is responsible for the [release to stockbilling](/release-to-stockbilling.md) process, which follows the drafting complete step
